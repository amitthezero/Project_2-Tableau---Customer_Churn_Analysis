## ** CUSTOMER CHURN ANALYSIS DASHBOARD**

---

## ** PROJECT OVERVIEW**
**This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer churn behavior, highlighting key drivers such as contract type, tenure, monthly charges, and customer segments. It enables data-driven decision-making to reduce churn and improve customer retention.**

---

## ** DATASET DESCRIPTION**
**The dataset represents telecom customer data, including demographic details, service subscriptions, billing information, and churn status. It is commonly used for churn prediction and retention strategy analysis.**

### **Key Features in Dataset:**
- **Customer ID:** Unique identifier for each customer  
- **Gender:** Male / Female  
- **Senior Citizen:** Indicates if the customer is a senior citizen  
- **Tenure:** Number of months the customer has stayed  
- **Contract Type:** Month-to-month, One year, Two year  
- **Monthly Charges:** Amount charged monthly  
- **Total Charges:** Total amount charged  
- **Services:** Internet, Phone, Online Security, Tech Support, etc.  
- **Churn:** Indicates whether the customer left (Yes/No)

---

## ** DASHBOARD INSIGHTS**

### **1. Churn Rate by Contract Type**
**Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn (~42.7%), while long-term contracts (one-year and two-year) significantly reduce churn.**

### **2. Churn Rate vs Tenure**
**Churn is highest in the early months (~0–12 months) and decreases as tenure increases, indicating higher risk among new customers.**

### **3. Revenue Lost vs Customer Segment**
**High-paying customers contribute the most to revenue loss, even though their churn percentage is lower compared to other segments.**

### **4. Churn by Tenure & Monthly Charges**
**Customers with high monthly charges and low tenure are more likely to churn, showing a strong correlation between cost sensitivity and early exit.**

### **5. Service Impact (Online Security & Tech Support)**
**Customers without online security and tech support services show significantly higher churn rates, indicating these services improve retention.**

---

## ** BUSINESS INSIGHTS**
- **Focus on converting month-to-month customers into long-term contracts**
- **Improve onboarding experience to reduce early churn**
- **Offer targeted retention strategies for high-paying customers**
- **Bundle services like tech support and online security to reduce churn**
- **Monitor high monthly charge customers closely**
